# Volume Activation Services
Volume Activation Services is a server role that enables you to automate and simplify the issuance and management of Microsoft software volume licenses for a variety of scenarios and environments. With Volume Activation Services, you can install and configure the Key Management Service \(KMS\) and Active Directory\-Based Activation.

# Checklist: Review and Manage a Signed Zone
[Checklist: Deploy DNSSEC](../Topic/Checklist--Deploy-DNSSEC.md) > [Checklist: Sign a Zone](../Topic/Checklist--Sign-a-Zone.md) > [Checklist: Distribute Trust Anchors](../Topic/Checklist--Distribute-Trust-Anchors.md) > [Checklist: Deploy DNSSEC Policies to DNS Clients](../Topic/Checklist--Deploy-DNSSEC-Policies-to-DNS-Clients.md) > **Checklist: Review and Manage a Signed Zone**  
  
This parent checklist includes links to subordinate checklists that help you complete the required tasks.  
  
Before you complete the tasks in this checklist, make sure that you have performed the prerequisite tasks in the parent checklist, such as reviewing conceptual information about DNSSEC and signing at least one DNS zone.  
  
Most of the tasks in this checklist are optional. Depending on your environment, you might have to perform some or all of these procedures.  
  
> [!NOTE]  
> Complete the tasks in this checklist in order. When a reference link takes you to a conceptual topic or to a subordinate checklist, return to this topic after you review the conceptual topic or after you complete the tasks in the subordinate checklist so that you can proceed with the remaining tasks in this checklist.

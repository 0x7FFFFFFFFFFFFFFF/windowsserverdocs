# Checklist: Sign a Zone
[Checklist: Deploy DNSSEC](../Topic/Checklist--Deploy-DNSSEC.md) > **Checklist: Sign a Zone**  
  
This checklist includes procedures to help you sign a zone with DNSSEC.  
  
Before you complete the tasks in this checklist, make sure you have performed the prerequisite tasks in the parent checklist, such as reviewing conceptual information about DNSSEC and deciding the deployment method and settings to use.  
  
> [!NOTE]  
> Complete the tasks in this checklist in order. When a reference link takes you to a conceptual topic or to a subordinate checklist, return to this topic after you review the conceptual topic or after you complete the tasks in the subordinate checklist so that you can proceed with the remaining tasks in this checklist.
